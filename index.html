Services

Walks

Our dog walks are the perfect way to let your pup sniff, investigate, and explore the world beyond their doorstep; And to potty, of course.

  • 30 minutes
  • waste pickup and disposal
  • fresh water
  • treats
  • text update with pictures
  • gps tracked walks

$20 per walk (1-2 dogs)

Visits

We offer a variety of options for home visits including regular drop in visits, extended visits, and overnight stays.

  • 30 minutes normal visit or 1 hour extended visit
  • food and water
  • potty break/freshen litter
  • waste pickup and disposal
  • play time
  • treats
  • administer medicine
  • extended visits include a walk

​ $20 per 30 minute visit (1-2 pets)
$35 per hour visit (1-2 pets)
$2 per additional pet per visit

Overnight Stays

  • 12 hour stay

  • play time
  • treats
  • administer medicine

$85 per overnight stay

Pet Taxi

Our pet taxi service is a convenient way to get your pet to and from all of their appointments, regardless of your busy schedule.

  • oneway drop off or pick up
  • roundtrip drop off and pick up

About Us

Peaceful Paws is a family owned and operated pet care service for the North Dallas area. We are driven by our admiration and love of dogs, as well as our belief that the bond between human and canine transcends the pet-owner relationship. Our commitment to trust, dependability and safety has made us welcome in homes (and backyards) all across the Dallas area. Whether you’re a busy breadwinner who wants to keep their furry family healthy and happy or you need an extra hand to help your pup to reach their full paw-tential. We believe that sometimes you need a helping hand to give a peaceful paw.
